The house rules

Every fuchs app is built on Fuchsbau — a shared design system and a shared ethos:

cloud_off
Local-firstYour data lives on your device. No accounts, no server, no analytics.
block
No ads, no dark patternsNothing lures, nags, or guilt-trips. Rewarding is fine; manipulating is not.
visibility
For all eyesDark & light themes, full font scaling, and dyslexia- and low-vision-friendly typefaces.
